He has served as Principal Second Violin with the Erie Philharmonic, the Youngstown Symphony, the Bay-Atlantic Symphony, and the Wheeling Symphony.</p>

            <p class="text-para">Mr. Moser has loved music ever since childhood when he would sit under the family piano listening to his parents and grandmother playing Beethoven, Chopin, and Rachmaninoff. During his education, he discovered a passion for conducting. Mr. Moser has been the Artistic Director/ Music Director of the Erie Jr. Philharmonic, the Mercyhurst Civic Orchestra, the Kutztown University Symphony Orchestra, the Wilmington Community Orchestra, and the Westminster Symphony Orchestra.</p>
            <p class="text-para">An advocate of lifelong learning, Mr. Moser has over the past 5 years, developed skills and training as both a piano technician and a string repair technician. He is currently the Luthier for Volkwein's Music in Pittsburgh. </p>
            <p class="text-para">Summers, Mr. Moser works as the Orchestra Program Director for the Philadelphia International Music Festival where he organizes and oversees up to ten orchestras reaching nearly 500 students. He also oversees all their various competition auditions, prescreening hundreds of auditions each year. Mr. Moser is an alum of the Pierre Monteux School & Music Festival, where he attended in 2018 as a Bernard Osher Conducting Scholar.</p>
