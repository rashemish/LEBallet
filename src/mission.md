      <p class="story-label">Community</p>
      <h3>Who We Are</h3>
      <p>
        Lake Erie Ballet Company is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to providing a high-quality
        pre-professional dance experience for dancers from all local studios. We welcome young artists
        throughout the region to join us in creating inspiring performances for the Erie community.
        Through collaboration with Erie County schools and regional arts organizations, we strive to
        enrich the cultural landscape and nurture the next generation of performers.
      </p>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="mission-story-panel mission-panel-two">
    <div class="mission-overlay-card overlay-right">
      <p class="story-label">Mentorship</p>
      <h3>Our Commitment</h3>
      <p>
        The Lake Erie Ballet Company (LEBC), beginning in 1957 as Erie Civic Ballet, is committed to
        promoting the diversity of talent in Erie County by building partnerships with local dance
        organizations and ensuring that all dancers who audition will participate in a positive and
        inspirational learning environment where skilled training and mentorship are offered through
        exposure to professional artistic directors, rehearsal directors, actors, costume and lighting
        designers and backstage technicians.
      </p>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="mission-story-panel mission-panel-three">
    <div class="mission-overlay-card overlay-left">
      <p class="story-label">Tradition</p>
      <h3>An Erie Tradition</h3>
      <p>
        Dancers will learn about the profession of dance through active participation in auditions and
        rehearsals that will culminate in performances of the annual Erie tradition of The Nutcracker.
      </p>
    </div>
