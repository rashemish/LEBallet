<section class="mission-content">
  <h1>Inspiring Movement. Enriching Our Community.</h1>

  <p class="mission-intro">
    This mission guides everything we do at Lake Erie Ballet Company.
  </p>

  <div class="mission-block">
    <p>
      Lake Erie Ballet Company is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to providing a high-quality
      pre-professional dance experience for dancers from local studios throughout the region. We welcome
      young artists to join us in creating inspiring performances for the Erie community. Through collaboration
      with Erie County schools and regional arts organizations, we strive to enrich the cultural landscape and
      nurture the next generation of performers.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="mission-block">
    <h2>Our Commitment</h2>
    <p>
      Founded in 1957 as Erie Civic Ballet, Lake Erie Ballet Company (LEBC) is committed to promoting the
      diversity of talent in Erie County by building partnerships with local dance organizations. All dancers
      who audition participate in a positive and inspirational learning environment where skilled training and
      mentorship are offered through exposure to professional artistic directors, rehearsal directors, designers,
      and backstage technicians.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="mission-block">
    <h2>An Erie Tradition</h2>
    <p>
      Dancers gain firsthand experience in the profession of dance through active participation in auditions and
      rehearsals that culminate in performances of the annual Erie tradition of The Nutcracker.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>
